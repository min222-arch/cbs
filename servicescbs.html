Tea Export Division

CBS Logistics Limited operates a structured tea export division, sourcing premium Kenyan highland teas and delivering institutional-grade supply solutions to Africa, the Middle East, Europe and Asia.

Our export workflow includes: • Factory sourcing & intake verification • Quality grading & lab testing • Export-grade packaging • Digital batch traceability • FOB & CIF structured trade execution

We handle bulk contracts, private label programs, and long-term institutional supply agreements.
