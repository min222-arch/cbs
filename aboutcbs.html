MORE ABOUT US

CBS Logistics Limited was established in 2019 as a logistics company supporting growers who required reliable transportation of agricultural produce to markets in good time and in the right condition. The company initially specialized in cold-chain refrigeration solutions, ensuring freshness and quality preservation. Over the years, CBS Logistics has expanded into multiple operational arms, strengthening the agricultural export value chain and enabling African produce to access international markets efficiently. Today, CBS Logistics integrates: • Agricultural production • Processing and value addition • Cold-chain and dry logistics • Export aggregation and distribution Our operations are driven by one purpose: connecting African farmers to the world.
